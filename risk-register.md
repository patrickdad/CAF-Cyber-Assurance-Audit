# Cyber Assurance Risk Register

This document captures key risks identified during the simulated CAF cyber assurance assessment.

The risks are aligned with the **NCSC Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF)** principles.

---

## Risk Register

| Risk ID | CAF Principle | Risk Description | Impact | Likelihood | Risk Rating | Recommendation |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|--------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| R1 | A1 Governance | Lack of clearly defined cyber security governance structure and security ownership. | High | Medium | High | Establish a formal cyber security governance structure with defined roles and responsibilities. |
| R2 | A2 Risk Management | Cyber security risks are not formally documented or reviewed regularly. | Medium | Medium | Medium | Implement a formal cyber risk register and conduct quarterly risk reviews. |
| R3 | A3 Asset Management | Incomplete inventory of IT assets and systems within the organisation. | High | Medium | High | Develop and maintain a centralised asset inventory covering all systems and devices. |
| R4 | A4 Supply Chain | Limited security due diligence performed on third-party suppliers. | Medium | Low | Medium | Introduce supplier security assessments and contractual security requirements. |

---

## Risk Rating Method

Risk ratings are determined using a simple qualitative matrix based on:

- **Impact** (Low / Medium / High)
- **Likelihood** (Low / Medium / High)

Risk scores help prioritise remediation activities.

---

## Purpose

This register demonstrates how cyber assurance assessments identify and document organisational cyber security risks aligned with recognised frameworks such as the **NCSC Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF)**.
