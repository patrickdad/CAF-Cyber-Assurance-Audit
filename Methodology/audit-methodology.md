Audit Methodology
This simulated audit was conducted using a governance and risk-based assessment approach aligned to the NCSC Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF).

The review focused on CAF Objective A – Managing Security Risk.

The following evaluation criteria were applied:

Governance oversight of cyber security
Cyber risk management processes
Asset visibility and classification
Supplier and third-party security assurance
Each area was assessed against CAF Indicators of Good Practice and assigned a maturity rating.
